Harmony’s UI color palette is derived from Intuit’s brand palette, consisting of Intuit blue and a selection of accent colors that compliment our brand.

 

Blues and grays represent approximately 85% of color distribution in the UI. Use for key UI elements like navigation, backgrounds, the stage, buttons, links, and text.

 

Green, yellow, orange, and red represent approximately 10% of the overall color distribution. Use for Insights, data visualization, alerts, and highlight states.
